-Upon approaching the elevator and determining the desired floor:
	-If the desired floor is higher than your current floor:
		-press the up (^) button 
	-If the desired floor is lower than your current floor:
		-press the down button 
	-Wait for the elevator to reach your floor, and enter when the doors open.  
-Upon entering, press the numbered button that corresponds with the floor number you would like to visit. The door will close on its own
	-If you need to hold the door:
		-press and hold the 'door open' button
	-If you are in a hurry and would like the door to close more quickly:
		-press the 'door close' button
-When you reach your desired floor, the doors will open and you may exit to continue your day!
